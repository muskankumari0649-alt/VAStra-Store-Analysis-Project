# 🛍️ Vastra Store Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Vastra Store Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an Excel-based data analytics project that analyzes retail sales data to uncover customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, product trends, and regional insights. The project transforms raw sales data into an interactive dashboard, enabling data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Analyze sales performance across different months.
- Understand customer demographics and buying behavior.
- Identify top-performing sales channels and product categories.
- Discover high-performing states and cities.
- Provide actionable business recommendations to improve sales.

## 📈 Key Insights

- March recorded the highest sales, followed by August, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- Women contributed **64%** of total sales, making them the primary customer segment.
- Customers aged **30–50 years** generated the highest revenue.
- **92%** of customer orders were successfully delivered.
- Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart generated the highest sales and order volume.
- Sets, Kurtas, and Western Dresses accounted for **88%** of total sales.
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh were the top-performing states.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Launch promotional campaigns before March and August to maximize seasonal sales.
- Target women aged 30–50 through personalized marketing campaigns.
- Strengthen partnerships with Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart.
- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Focus marketing efforts on top-performing cities and states.
